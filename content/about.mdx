## Supporters

PROCEED has been chosen by the [Federal Ministry of Education and Research (BMBF)](http://www.bmbf.de/) to be funded within the Software Campus initiative. 
The Software Campus program integrates research and practical management experience in order to train researcher  to become managers and entrepreneurs with an excellent IT background.
